About me

À propos de moi

Curious and eager for knowledge, the profession of web developer logically presented itself to me in 2020. It's with enthusiasm and conviction that I have completed my retraining in this field.

I am a pragmatic developer who favors efficiency and consistency. Web development is a set of techniques. To have an effective site or application, the techniques used must be good.
